who am i?
hey there, i'm marc. a web developer from Lancashire, UK.
my journey to becoming a web dev
my journey started back in school, where i created a very basic Minesweeper clone, using excel and vba. having established myself as the most popular kid for IT lessons, i then used my spare time to re-create all the classic greats from the 80's like Pacman, Frogger and Galaga.
my coding career then took a break as i entered into a career of pension administration. progressing through team after team, i never truly found a place that could satisfy my desire to express my creativity and provide a challenge to enable my skills to be used.
in early 2012 i finally found my home and was able to put my previous 'coding' experience to use in a data analytics and reporting function. i quickly realised that my excel and vba knowledge was nothing compared to the power of databases and SQL
i immersed myself in learning everything i could about them. shortly after putting my learning into practice, i came across an article online about creating a reporting suite using HTML and after many hours of frustrated rubber duck debugging (mine is called Kevin), i finally published my first ever 'website'.
as my pension administration career progressed into management, and the technology available to my companies became available; i was thrown into the project management lifecycle of implementing new software.
working with a variety of methodologies including agile, scrum and waterfall; i finally felt at home in the fast-paced environment of software development; and while my coding knowledge left little to be desired at the time, i knew this was the career for me!
i began focussing all my spare time into learning everything i could about how to code. fate had other ideas for me as i was given a promotion in my pension administration career, now becoming a head of department. this afforded me more hands on experience of the project lifecycle and managing a team of developers in high profile projects.
fate again twisted my journey and in late summar 2019, my career within pension administration ceased. i took time with my family, my friends and myself, but i knew my next career would be as a web developer. i began focussing all my efforts on expaning my knowledge. i started creating websites for local businesses in my area and have created multiple android games available on the Google Play Store.
