who am i.
Hey there!! I'm Marc, a web developer.
where am i.
Based in the Fylde Coast in the county of Lancashire, UK.
what am i about.
I'm a passionate, motivated and highly driven person who loves to create simple, fun and exciting applications and website that people enjoy using.
why web dev.
My intrigue for coding began in High School, where I was fascinated by the simplicity of code, where the world is made of one's and zero's. My first experience 'coding' was by building a minesweeper game in excel using VBA.
Ever since then, i've always held a strong interest in the development of technologies and the way the world of coding has adapted. Being a part of that world, and using my creativity to develop websites, applications and more for people to enjoy is something i've wanted since i finished that minesweeper excel game.
how.
My life after High School didn't thrust me into the life of coding, and a career in Pension Administration is what found me. Whilst not ideal, it has rewarded me with a wealth of experience which aligns with the world of development.
Working for Public Sector Pension Schemes, I have been heavily involved in more than 100 projects, ranging from small system changes, to brand new system implementation. The project lifecycle of Agile, Scrum and Waterfall methodologies are imprinted in my brain like the lyrics to the Circle of Life.
My involvement and management of projects, coupled with my team leadership experience has given me skills and knowledge that can't be learnt from a book.
In mid 2019, i realised i wasn't happy in the career that life had chosen for me. I decided that i wanted a change, and began teaching myself everything i could about web development. Using the internet,books and former colleagues as mentors; the more i learnt, the more fascinated i become.
In January 2020, I released my first creation to the Google Play Store, a simple game called "Keepie Uppies", pulling together all that i had learned about Object Oriented Programming
